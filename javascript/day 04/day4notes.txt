Datatypes
===========
-Types of data we are dealing with

2 types: primitive and non-primitive

primitives:
      number: 4,4.5, -2.3
      string: 'sachin', '15', 'true'
      boolean: true/false
      undefined: undefined
      bigint
      symbol

non-primitive: Object


primitives vs non-primitives
==============================
primitives are immutable and non-primitives are mutable
primitives are compared by value and non-primitives are compared by address
